onchain score

Our score checker an onchain identity scanner on base, taking into account up to 3 wallets connected to each Farcaster account.


The score depends on users' onchain activity: tokens, nfts, wallet history and so on.


After the token launch, Leaderboard and SDK will allow users to use data from score checker for building products, networks, and novel distribution systems.

Base score is given based on the following criteria:

  • Base official NFT
  • FC-native collections
  • Base tokens
  • Ens, transactions, wallet’s history 
  • Coinbase Attestations
  • Activity on Farcaster
  • Popular base collections
